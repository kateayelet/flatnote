FlatNote

Plain markdown notes that sync across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

FlatNote is a markdown notes app for people who want their writing to stay plain, portable, and theirs. Type markdown and watch it format as you go. Every note is a plain .md file in your own iCloud, with no accounts, no tracking, and no lock-in.

Features

  • Live formatting that hides the syntax and reveals it on the line you edit
  • Tappable checkboxes, lists, headings, emphasis, links, and quotes
  • Find in a note, and search across your whole library
  • iCloud sync across your devices, with a local fallback
  • Export and share any note as markdown
